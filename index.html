Switch Keyring

The Switch Keyring is a ring of switch enthusiasts that exchange physical switch keychains over mail or in-person at meetups.

Specification

A switch keyring is defined as follows:

  • Must have a 5mm+ diameter hole no more than 10mm away from one edge (for use on a 20mm+ keyring).
  • Must have a switch footprint.
  • Must be no larger than 30mm x 80mm in size.
